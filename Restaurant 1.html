The Heritage

Culinary Artistry in the Heart of Ekiti

Founded on the principle of "Spices without Borders," SpiceRoute Kitchen blends the smoky depths of local wood-fired cooking with modern gastronomic techniques.

Our Executive Chef, with over 15 years of international experience, sources every ingredient from local Ado-Ekiti farmers to ensure peak freshness and community growth.
